About Us

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Skopje, our 3D printing service has grown into a hub of creativity and innovation.
With sponsorship from our partners Tehnomarket & Anhoch, we’ve been able to expand our capabilities and drive advancements in the 3D printing industry.
Our facility houses over 10 specialized printers, ready to bring your ideas to life with precision and speed—whether it’s prototyping, custom parts, or unique designs.
